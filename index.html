Our Story

A legacy of taste, heritage & prestige.

Nestled in the heart of Kimihurura, Glimpse Hotel & Restaurant offers an exceptional culinary experience around a distinguished table in the capital.

Our beautifully designed rooms and suites offer stunning views and plush accommodations, ensuring a restful retreat whether you're here for business or leisure.

From handcrafted cocktails to sizzling tandoor specials, every dish and drink is crafted to perfection, making Glimpse a destination where every moment becomes a memory.
